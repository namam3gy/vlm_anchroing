## Decision

**Borderline — leaning accept for EMNLP Main on the novelty axis, conditional on three targeted fixes.** The work has *genuine* novelty: (i) the **(a − m) digit-pixel paired-inpaint contrast** is a clever calibration-design move with no direct prior precedent in either the typographic-attack or activation-steering literature; (ii) the **continuous confidence gradient → wrong/correct binary projection** reframe of anchoring as a graded-pull phenomenon (§4.4 6-bin) is a genuinely new behavioral lens that no prior LLM or VLM anchoring paper supplies; (iii) the **multi-layer-redundancy → single-direction-failure prospective prediction** chain (§5.2 → §6.4) and the **multi-direction subspace projection passing a formally-defined 4-clause free-lunch criterion under multiplicity correction** (§6.2 + §7) is the EMNLP-Main differentiator the paper banks on, and it largely holds up. **But three positioning issues remain that any informed reviewer will catch:** (CRIT-N1) §6.5 Table 8 still claims the 5-baseline panel passage *uniquely* (line 422 "권장 — 4-clause free-lunch 통과", caption "Bold = 본 작업") without surfacing in the table itself that CAA and ITI are *missing rows* reduced to footnote-text only — the structural-reduction argument is plausible but a referee will ask "why not a single empirical CAA-at-K=1 row to settle it"; (CRIT-N2) the auxiliary observation γ-β reasoning amplification is correctly hedged (N=1 × N=1 existence proof) in §1.5 but the abstract's `correct-base df ratio ×12.7 point estimate` is still a load-bearing number with no CI, no ratio-of-ratios bootstrap, and a denominator the paper itself says is small in §8.2 — a hostile reviewer who Ctrl-Fs to "×12.7" in the abstract will not find the hedge until §1.4 / §4.5; (CRIT-N3) the §1.5 (3) "routing vs integration synthesis" is labeled as the paper's *theoretical* contribution but Round 1 honestly downgraded it to *post-hoc synthesis* of §5.2 + §5.3 + §6.4 with only the §4.6 γ-β bridge as a prospective leg — and the §4.6 leg is *qualitative only, quantitative interlock deferred* (§4.6 Insight 2 + §8.2). A theoretical contribution whose sole prospective verification is qualitative-and-partial is closer to a *useful framework* than a *load-bearing theory* — the contribution language ("이론적 기여") needs to be softened to match.

**Critically, the question the user raised — "is this an experiment-log dump or a top-tier paper?" — is resolved on the writing axis (Round 2 cleanup successful) but remains *unresolved on the contribution-framing axis*. The current §1.5 has four parallel contributions of roughly equal claimed weight (framework / cross-dataset evidence / synthesis / mitigation), which is *the structural fingerprint of an experiment catalog*, not a focused EMNLP-Main paper. Top-tier papers usually have one central claim with 2-3 supporting findings. The paper has the substance for that compression — but the framing has not been compressed.** See "Single central contribution audit" below.

## Recommended venue tier

**EMNLP 2026 Main, borderline — accept conditional on CRIT-N1/N2/N3 fixes; otherwise EMNLP Findings.** The paper *does* clear the project.md §"What EMNLP Main demands" bar on three of four criteria: (a) mechanistic / causal analysis ✓ via §5.2 multi-layer-redundancy ablation + §5.3 OneVision dataset-dependent peak + §4.6 γ-β residual sign-reversal; (b) mitigation method ✓ via E4 + E6 with the 4-clause free-lunch criterion and 6-benchmark capability preservation; (c) novel methodological framework ✓ via (a − m) paired-inpaint contrast as calibration substrate + multi-direction subspace SVD projection — these are genuinely new application-level moves; (d) genuinely surprising scientific claim ≈ partial, via γ-β reasoning amplification (auxiliary observation, N=1 × N=1) and the continuous-confidence-gradient reframe of wrong/correct asymmetry. Strength on (a) + (b) + (c) is what pushes this above Echterhoff 2024 (Findings) and toward Weng 2024 (Main).

**However**, the *theoretical-contribution load-bearing* check (Round-1 CRIT-1 cleanup) leaves the §5.4 framework at "post-hoc synthesis + qualitative-only prospective leg" — i.e., the *theoretical* contribution is now the weakest of the four. That's a Findings-pattern signal. The two contributions that *do* carry Main-tier weight are (a − m) paired-inpaint contrast and 4-clause free-lunch criterion — both methodological, both falsifiable, both genuinely new — and the paper would be served by restructuring §1.5 to make those the *headline* contributions with the synthesis and the cross-dataset evidence as *supporting findings*.

Mid-confidence venue verdict: **EMNLP Main if CRIT-N1/N2/N3 fixed + §1.5 restructured to single-headline form; EMNLP Findings if §1.5 stays at 4-parallel-contributions and the synthesis is still labeled "이론적 기여"; NeurIPS Main unlikely** because NeurIPS weights formal theoretical contributions more heavily and the post-hoc synthesis would be challenged harder there.
